My Approach

Substance is Non-Negotiable.
Content is King.

Precision in Research

Whether it's breaking news or B2B content, I start at the source. I solve problems by digging for the "why" before writing the "what." Accuracy is the foundation of trust.

Adaptability in Voice

The medium dictates the message. I translate goals into the specific language of the platform—urgent radio tone or SEO-optimized long-form articles.

Collaborative Synergy

In TV and Radio, nothing is a solo act. I approach teamwork with a "production-first" mindset, ensuring seamless integration with editors and stakeholders.
